A monitor stand riser might seem like a small addition, but it can transform your posture and free up valuable desk space. By lifting your screen to eye level, you reduce neck strain and create room for a keyboard, documents, or clutter. When choosing one, focus on height, weight capacity, footprint, and build quality; your monitor should sit at a comfortable viewing angle without wobbling. Budget models often use molded plastic, while mid-range and premium risers feature solid wood or metal. Some include cable management or a storage drawer. The key is to match the riser to your monitor size and your desk layout. Below we highlight three options that cover different needs without breaking the bank.

Best for small desks

When desk real estate is tight, a compact riser like the Wali Compact Monitor Riser can free up space without sacrificing stability. The raised wooden build also provides enough clearance to route cables out of sight, making it a solid choice for a tidy workspace. Our pick: Aothia Wooden Monitor Stand.

Our verdict

For most people, the VIVO Monitor Riser offers the best balance of price and function. It lifts your monitor reliably and leaves room underneath for storage.
