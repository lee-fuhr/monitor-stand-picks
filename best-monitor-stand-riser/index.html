Quick answer

The VIVO Adjustable Monitor Riser is our top pick for its sturdy build, tool-free height adjustment, and excellent cable management.

If you prefer a wood-grain look with built-in storage, the HUANUO Wood Monitor Riser is a strong runner-up.

A good monitor stand riser does more than just lift your screen to eye level, it saves your neck, shoulders, and posture over a long workday. By raising your monitor a few inches, you can align the top bezel with your line of sight, reducing strain and helping you stay comfortable. Beyond ergonomics, many risers also free up desk space for a keyboard or documents and tidy up cables. When choosing a riser, consider the height adjustment range (fixed vs. adjustable), platform size, and whether it fits your monitor’s weight and footprint. Materials matter too: steel is sturdy but heavy, wood adds warmth, and plastic is lightweight but less durable. Think about your desk’s layout and whether you need extra features like drawers, USB ports, or sit-stand versatility.
